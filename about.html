About Us

A Brief History

Ganesh Granites was established in 1971, by Mr. Prakash Bafna.

Established in 1971, the company's initial focus was to supply wholesale granites and marbles locally to their consumers at the best rates.
From 1985 onwards, the company started to exporting granites and marbles globally. The main countries where the company exported were, USA, Germany, Singapore, Malaysia, Sri Lanka, United Kingdom.
Between 1985 - 2000 the company was an "Export Oriented Unit (EOU)".
From then on there was no stopping, the company went on to supply granites to leading builders such as L&T, Jain Housing, ECCI, etc.
They have also supplied goods to Delhi Metro, Changi Airport(Singapore) and Hyderabad Race Course.
In the year 2012, Ganesh Granites expanded in to the Faber & Franke Products by inaugurating Faber Galleria. Faber Galleria has all the faber products, from chimneys to cooktops to hobs. A wide range of Franke products are also available.
